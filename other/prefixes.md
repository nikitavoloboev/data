# Prefixes & notations
Prefixes and notations I use for various apps and tools and their meanings.

## SnippetsLab
- <> - User input

## 1Password
- f: - Family
- ssh: - SSH

## Pixave
- w. - Wallpapers
- r. - Reactions
- s. - Scans
- rg. - Reaction GIFs

## Contacts
- () - Nickname
- \# - Tags

## PDFs
- r. - Research papers
- d. - Documents
- b. - Books
- s. - Personal

## Keyboard Maestro
- w: - Safari websites open
- g: - Global macros
- c: - Chrome websites open
- open: - Open applications
- e: - Examples
- {} - What should be in my clipboard
- [] - What state the macro should be in
- <> - What link should be in my browser

- r. - Private workflows

## Safari
- w: - Websites
- s: - Personal
